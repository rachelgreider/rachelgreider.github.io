Individual Deterrents to Voting

These are circumstances that keep people from being able to go vote. Some laws are put in place to exploit these struggles.

Voter Roll Purging

People are rarely notified that they are no longer registered to vote so that they cannot cast a regular ballot on election day.

Prison Gerrymandering

Because of gerrymandering, specifically prison gerrymandering, each vote cast does not have an equal weight during an election.
