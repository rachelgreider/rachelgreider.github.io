Anonymous

Anonymous is a hypothetical movie about a girl stuck and bored with her routine until she makes some drastic changes in her life.

I started this title sequence by thinking about a stereotypical daily routine which starts the morning with caffeine (coffee), starts the afternoon with a lower dose of caffeine (tea), and starts the evening with alcohol (wine).

This routine brought me to my mood board of watercolor drawings and handwritten lettering. I created this video with a combination of watercolor backgrounds and vector forgrounds.
