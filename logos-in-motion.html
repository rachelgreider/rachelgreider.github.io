Logos in Motion

The goal of this exercise was to present two already established brands in a new way.

Lush's brand loves to utilize their products alongside the black and white logo. I took macro-photos of several of their products and filmed several bath-bombs to achieve these two videos.

Soundcloud is known for obscure artists so the two animations are differnent than a normal music streaming platform ad.
