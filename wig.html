Wig Promotional Animation

Wig is an HBO documentary about drag queens in New York City. It starred many queens, including Lady Bunny and Neil Patrick Harris, who also co-produced it.

I created these animated videos for Instagram, HBO.com, and Twitter to promote the Wig documentary release. This was my first time turning a pre-existing static design into an animated asset.
(This is also my first design to be seen by the public!)
