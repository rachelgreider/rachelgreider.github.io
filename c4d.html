Cinema 4D Experience

This summer I have been experiementing with materiality of objects within Cinema 4D. I started by modeling a butterfly then applying several different materials to that butterfly.

I created this miniature scene as a home for a mouse (who will eventually be modeled too) Within this scene I learned the basics of creating a landscape and modeling simple furniture.

I am starting to get into more detailed modeling and material making techniques. I have utilized the modeling techniques to create the cheese wedge and the burro's tail succulent. I have experiemented with different materials on every model.
